PES3-Disc — helper for launching PS3-format discs in RPCS3 (your own install).

YOU MUST:
- Own any physical disc you decrypt or copy.
- Comply with copyright and anti-circumvention laws in your country.
- NOT redistribute, upload, or sell decrypted or copied game files.

PES3-Disc does not include games, keys, or RPCS3. It is not affiliated with Sony,
PlayStation, or the RPCS3 developers.

Persistent cache keeps decrypt output on your disk for faster replay; you remain
responsible for those files.
